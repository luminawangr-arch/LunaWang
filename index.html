About

Two skill sets, one workflow

I'm a Financial Management student at the Shanghai University of Finance and Economics (SUFE), with an exchange semester at CUHK. My equity research runs along two tracks.

In professional roles I've covered innovative pharmaceuticals and the building-materials sector — from fundamental analysis and pipeline evaluation to valuation modeling and in-depth reports adopted for institutional roadshows. What ties the two together is the engineering side. I taught myself to build the tools I wanted analysts to have — most recently a full-stack equity research platform that pulls live market data, computes technical indicators, and generates simulated portfolio recommendations end to end.

Things I built

Independent · Full-stack

Virtual Stock Portfolio Analysis Platform

An end-to-end equity research platform that screens news- and policy-driven sectors, analyzes individual stocks across fundamentals and technicals, and generates risk-adjusted, simulated portfolio recommendations — then monitors holdings and flags position-adjustment signals.

I designed and built it from scratch: a Python / FastAPI backend serving live A-share market data through AKShare, technical indicators (MA, MACD, RSI) computed in pandas, LLM APIs wired in for automated sector and stock analysis, portfolios persisted in SQLite, and a React + ECharts front end for interactive dashboards.
